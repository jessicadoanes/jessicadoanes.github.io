About

Jessica describes herself as a data specialist. She is passionate about providing communities with the resources to utilize data to understand their power within community development. Jessica is currently an Evaluation Consultant for Neighborhood Revitalization Habitat for Humanity International (HFHI). Before joining HFHI in 2018, she received her Master’s degree from Emory University and assisted with the evaluation of the CARE-Cargill partnership from 2008-2018 at CARE USA. Jessica’s past work involves consulting with academic institutions, nonprofits, government agencies, and international organizations on social, economic, and political issues. Her specialty is working with communities and organizations to employ systems thinking to develop data and measurement systems to inform decision-making, program design, data analysis, and data visualization and storytelling.
